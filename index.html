The founders

Corentin Rodriguez

From Bayonne, Corentin Rodriguez was at Audencia Business School. Majored in sports events management, he is today in MYC Capital team to organize all the events to promote our company. He is also the head manager of the European branch of MYC Capital.

Matthieu Oung

From Paris, Matthieu Oung majored in Corporate Finance at Audencia Business School. After five years in M&A in Lazard, he decided to create MYC Capital with his friends. He is the current financial director of MYC Capital is the head manager of the Asian branch.

Yann Pretto

Yann Pretto was born in Thionville. After majoring in consulting at Audencia Business School, he worked for the greatest consulting companies. Last year, he decided to join MYC Capital to achieve his dream : discover Asia through its business. He is the head manager of East Asia and Oceania branch.
